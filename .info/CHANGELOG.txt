SAE v0.9.2.5
# Fixes
- Negate Prayers could be added to Protect prayers, making enemies have a 0% chance to hit. This has been fixed, and the Negate prayers have been changed (see Changes).
- Refined Saltpetre is used to purchase (and later, make) Gunpowder, yet required level 30 Smithing, despite Bronze firearms (level 13) existing. The Smithing level required has been reduced to 15, and the Mining level reduced to 20.
- Karil's Offhand Crossbow could not be obtained. This has been fixed.

# Changes
- Negate Prayers changed to accommodate a gamebreaking bug. 
  - Before: 85% chance to dodge Magic/Melee/Ranged attacks
  - After: +5% Flat Resistance against Magic/Melee/Ranged and +50% Magic/Melee/Ranged Evasion
- Removed Wand of Death and Polymorph and their effects (Death Magic and Polymorph) from the game.
  - Two reasons: 
    1. They were just far too powerful in their states. One could simply equip the Wand of Death or Polymorph and defeat any monster, including The Herald, with no chance of them ignoring the effect. The effects themselves, when given to a monster, were also too specific and fight-ending, they just didn't feel fair to include.
    2. They don't fit my vision for the expansion. They're a little too weird. The other wands are OK, as they use existing effects and certain items do similar things.
