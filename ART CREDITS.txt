AI DISCLAIMER
This project does not and will not utilize generative AI for ANY of its art, code, data, or anything that would otherwise be expected of a real human to make. Any AI used is strictly for the purposes of simplifying the colours of non-vector images used to aid in manually transforming them into simple flat vectors. Non-vector images are then painstakingly hand-traced and coloured using GIMP after such processes are used. Any AI programs or tools used and their purposes in this project are listed under the AI TOOLS section.

AI TOOLS
ChatGPT—to ASSIST (emphasis on assist) in performing redundant and/or tedious tasks, such as transferring data to a Markdown Table for the Wiki, or creating multiple similar JSON objects that would take two to three times as long to do by hand, and be more prone to errors.
Vectorizer.ai and Vectormagic.ai—to reduce and simplify the colours of non-vector images to speed up the process of manually colouring and shaping images into 2d flat vectors that visually fit into Melvor.
